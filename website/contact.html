Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept PhD students?

I occasionally co-supervise PhD students in the areas of educational technology, AI in education, and immersive learning. Please reach out with your research interests and background to discuss potential opportunities.

Can I visit your lab?

Visits to our CAVE facility and VR/AR lab can be arranged for researchers, educators, and industry partners. Please contact me to schedule a visit.

Do you offer consulting services?

Yes, I provide consulting for educational institutions and edtech companies on implementing AI, VR/AR, and robotics in educational settings. Please reach out to discuss your specific needs.

What languages do you speak?

I am fluent in English, Mandarin Chinese, and Cantonese, and can conduct research, presentations, and collaborations in all three languages.
