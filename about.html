About Me

Passionate about transforming education through technology.

Biography

I am currently an Educational Development Officer at the Centre for Learning, Teaching and Technology, the Education University of Hong Kong. With over a decade of experience in educational technology, I have dedicated my career to exploring how emerging technologies can transform and enhance learning experiences.

My journey in EdTech began with a strong foundation in engineering, which I obtained from Beijing Normal University, Zhuhai. This technical background, combined with my Master's degree in AI and Educational Technology from EdUHK, has given me a unique perspective on the intersection of technology and pedagogy.

Currently, I am pursuing my Doctorate in Education at EdUHK, where my research focuses on the integration of generative AI and immersive technologies in educational settings. I am particularly interested in how these technologies can serve as cognitive scaffolds to support student learning and engagement.
